There are 4 mains types of element bending.

  • Waterbending,is the hydrokinetic ability to control water in all of its various forms. Water is the element of change. This type of bending is used by the people of the Water Tribe, who are divided into the Southern, Northern, and lesser-known Foggy Swamp tribes. The moon is the source of power in waterbending, and the original waterbenders learned to bend by observing how the moon pushed and pulled the tides.

  • Earthbending, is the geokinetic ability to manipulate earth and rock in all their various forms, prevalent in the Earth Kingdom and the United Republic. Earth is the element of substance, while the people of the Earth Kingdom are diverse, strong, and enduring. Oma and Shu were the first earthbenders to learn this art from the badgermoles.

  • Firebending, is the pyrokinetic ability to control and produce fire. It is unique among the bending arts, as it is the only one in which the performer can generate the element. The first human firebenders live prevelant among the Sun Warriors and the Fire Nation. Firebending draws its power from the sun, and the first human firebenders derived their firebending techniques from the dragons.

  • Airbending, is the aerokinetic ability to control and manipulate air. The peaceful Air Nomads utilized this type of bending in their everyday lives. Air is the element of freedom. The Air Nomads detached themselves from worldly problems and concerns; finding peace and freedom was the key to solving their difficulties in life. The first airbenders learned their art from the flying bison.
